Know the Facts

What is it?

Environmental sustainability refers to the responsible management and conservation of natural resources and ecosystems to meet current human needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It emphasizes the balance between environmental, social, and economic factors to ensure the long-term health of the planet.

Go to Goals

UN's Sustainable Development Goals

The Sustainable Development Goals, created by the United Nations, are a set of principles that aim to preserve peace and prosperity for people and the planet. They serve as a framework for volunteers, nonprofits, and other organizations to work towards a sustainable future for our planet. Hover over the cards below to learn more!

No Poverty

End poverty in all its forms worldwide. Ensure everyone has access to basic needs and economic opportunities.

No Poverty

Aims to end malnutrition by ensuring everyone has access to safe, sufficent, and nutritious food.

Good Health

Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for everyone at all ages, focusing on reducing diseases and improving healthcare access.

Quality Education

Provide inclusive and equitable quality education, ensuring lifelong learning opportunities for all.

Gender Equality

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girs, ending discrimination and violence against them.

Clean Water

Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for everyone.

Affordable Clean Energy

Expand access to affordable, reliable, and modern energy, focusing on renewable sources. Improve energy efficency.

Decent Work

Promote inclusive and sustainable economic growth, employment, and decent work for all. It strives to create job opportunities.

Triple I

Build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation.

Reduced Inequalities

Reduce inequality within and among countries. It seeks to improve economic and social inclusion for all, regardless of age, gender, race, disability, etc.

Sustainable Cities

Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient, and sustainable. This goal promotes sustainable urbanization, affordable housing, etc.

Responsible Consumption

Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns. It aims to reduce waste, promote efficient use of resources.

Climate Action

Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. This goal focuses on reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

Life Below Water

Conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources for sustainable development.

Life on Land

Protect, restore, and promote the sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems.

Peace, Justice

Promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provide access to justice for all, and build effective, accountable institutions.

Partnership

Strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the global partnership for sustainable development.

Our Vision

Address the lack of opportunities for the average person to learn about environmental sustainability and how they can help the environment.

Our Mission

Provide a platform for people to learn about how they use natural resources in their daily lives and how they can aid environmental activism.
